Our Mission

The kids-Zone is an online place of learning and fun for the kids. It has been designed in such a manner that it not only catches the attention but also opens many doors to the kids that they feel themselves in a playland. We have provided huge stuff for them to learn , starting from Alphabets and ending on Games and differents activities.

Our Website

It has been divided into 5 sections i.e.Maths, Science, English, Games, Stories and Rhymes.

The Maths section includes counting with or without words, times table, addition, subtraction, multiplication, division, and shapes lessons. This section is supported by JavaScript to create interactive mathematical activities by taking input from the user and validating the given input by showing results of correct or incorrect.

The Science section comprises of information related to the scientific reasoning of a kid's life. A kid's exploration starts right from his/her own surrounding objects and pictures or even him/herself. Therefore, this section begins with basic concepts of science like what is science and vocabulary , parts of body names, animals, birds, flowers , fruits and vegetables.

The English section includes lessons on ABC , Months Name , Days Name , Colors Name , Prayers and Grammar. The English Section has been design in order to help the kids learn and enhance their English Vocabulary.

The Games section is an online portal of educational games. The kids enter this section and find many games which are not only there to entertain them but also remind those lessons and also put into practice which they just learned while navigating throught the pages of the Kids Zone.

The Stories Section is a fairy world for the kids. It contains different types of stories like Fairy Tales , Ghost Tales , King and the Queen Stories and many more.
